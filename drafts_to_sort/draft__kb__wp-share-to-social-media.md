## ğŸ“ Writing Rules

**Universal Elements:**
```
[Hook emoji+problem] 1 line
[Solutions/benefits] 2-3 lines  
[CTA + link] 1 line
[2-3 hashtags]
```

**Platform Differences:**
- **Facebook:** Casual, emoji-heavy, personal CTA
- **LinkedIn:** Professional, skills-focused, collaboration
- **Twitter:** Ultra-concise, technical keywords, hashtags

**Character Math:**
```
Twitter: 280 - 23(link) - 20(hashtags) = 237 content
LinkedIn: Write Twitter Ã— 10
Facebook: Write LinkedIn Ã— 20 (but keep punchy)
```
