## ğŸ§  The reality of hiring

Modern hiring teams face massive application loads. Often, HR or recruiting teams juggle multiple open roles, administrative tasks, and compliance documentation simultaneously.  
Providing personalized feedback for each applicant adds legal risk and consumes time that organizations rarely allocate for post-interview follow-up.  

Furthermore, written feedback creates liability. Even a well-intended comment can be misinterpreted, forwarded publicly, or used in disputes, discouraging companies from offering transparency.
